<div class="heading">
  <div class="name">Multiply</div>
  <div class="command">R←X×Y</div>
</div>

`Y` may be any numeric array.  `X` may be any numeric array.  `R` is the arithmetic product of `X` and `Y`.

This function is also known as Times.

# Example
```apl
      3 2 1 0 × 2 4 9 6
6 8 9 0
```
