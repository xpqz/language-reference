<div class="heading">
  <div class="name">Add</div>
  <div class="command">R←X+Y</div>
</div>

`Y` must be numeric.  `X` must be numeric.  `R` is the arithmetic sum of `X` and `Y`.  `R` is numeric. This function is also known as Plus.

# Examples
```apl
      1 2 + 3 4
4 6
 
      1 2 + 3,⊂4 5
4  6 7
 
      1J1 2J2 + 3J3
4J4 5J5
 
      ¯5 + 4J4 5J5
¯1J4 0J5
```
